About Me

My name is Meilu Zhu. Now, I'm a Research Assistant in AIM Group, Department of Electrical Engineering, City University of Hong Kong (CityU) and advised by Prof. Yixuan Yuan. I received the M.Eng. degree in Computer Science and Technology from Shenzhen University in 2019, supervised by Prof. Daming Shi who is a member of Research Institute for Future Media Computing. During 2019-2020, I worked as a Research Assistant in Sing Group of Prof.Kai Chen in The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST).

My research interests are Deep Learning, Machine Learning, Computer Vision, Medical Image Analysis and Facial Landmark Detection (Face Alignment).
