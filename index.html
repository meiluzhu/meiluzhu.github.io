About Me

I'm currently a last-year Ph.D. student in City University of Hong Kong (CityUHK) and supervised by Prof. Yixuan Yuan, CUHK. Before this, I received the Master's degree from Shenzhen University (SZU). My research interests include Large Models, Computer Vision, Medical Image Analysis and Federated Learning.

I would welcome the opportunity to explore potential research collaborations. Please feel free to reach out if any aspects of my work align with your current research interests.
