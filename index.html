About Me

My name is Meilu Zhu. I'm a third-year postgraduate student and will graduate from Shenzhen University, China, in July 2019. Now, I'm also a member of Research Institute for Future Media Computing and advised by Professor Daming Shi.

My research interests are facial landmark detection (face alignment), deep learning and machine learning.

Recently, I am looking for a suited university for doctoral study.
